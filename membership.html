Good to Know

Membership Details.

Will membership prices ever change?

Membership pricing may evolve over time as services expand. If changes happen, active members are notified clearly and in advance.

What if I don't book a facial one month?

Your monthly fee always banks as credit toward any service or product. Credits never expire while your membership is active. You can also gift your facial credit to a friend or family member.

Can I pause my membership?

Yes. Members can pause for up to 3 months per calendar year — consecutive or in separate increments. Just let us know in advance and we'll take care of the rest.

How do I cancel?

We require one full membership term's written notice (30 days). After your first 30 days, you're free to cancel at any time with notice. Any banked credits expire 90 days after cancellation.

Do elevated treatments count toward my membership?

Elevated treatments — microneedling, laser, and XERF — are not included in the monthly facial credit, but members receive significant discounts (15–25% depending on tier). The Club tier also banks one elevated treatment credit per quarter.

Can I upgrade my tier later?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle. Downgrades also apply from the next billing cycle.

Is there a minimum commitment?

We ask for a 30-day minimum before cancellation. After that, membership is month-to-month with 30 days' written notice to cancel. No long-term contracts.
