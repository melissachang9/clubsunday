The Beginning

Born on a Sunday

Club Sunday started with a question: why does good skincare have to feel so clinical? We wanted to build a space where monthly facials feel less like appointments and more like catching up with your favourite people.

What started as a small studio has become a membership-based skincare club — specialising in advanced facials and bio-stimulatory treatments designed to deliver visible, lasting results across every skin tone. This is not a sterile clinical office. This is not a fluffy spa with candles and no results. Club Sunday lives in the sweet spot.

"From Routine to Ritual. Welcome to The Club."
— Club Sunday
What We're About

Our Core Values

Results You Can See

We don't do "relaxation-only" facials. Every treatment is designed around real, visible results using advanced technologies that activate your skin's own healing response.

Built for Every Skin Tone

Every technology we adopt, every product we carry, and every protocol we design is vetted for safety and efficacy across the full spectrum of skin tones. Inclusive isn't a marketing word here — it's our clinical standard.

Clean & Intentional

We're selective about what touches your skin. No harsh chemicals, no filler ingredients, no compromise. Every product is chosen with the same care you'd want for your own medicine cabinet.

Innovation with Integrity

We source the most advanced technologies from South Korea, Japan, France, and beyond — but only adopt what's been clinically validated and proven safe across diverse skin types.

Community First

This isn't just a clinic — it's a club. A place where skincare meets community and every visit feels like your monthly reset with people who genuinely care about your skin.
